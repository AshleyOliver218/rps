Rules of the game:
Lets play "Rock, Paper, Scissors!" In this classic game "Rock" beats "Scissors", "Paper" beats "Rock", and "Scissors" beats "Paper". When you choose between "Rock", "Paper", or "Scissors", click on the picture of your preferred item. Whichever item you choose will have a blue dotted box around it marking your selection. Whichever item the computer chooses will have a red dotted box around it. If both you and the computer choose the same item, it will have a purple dotted box around it. Whichever item, chosen by either you or the computer that is the winning item, will be displayed the largest on the screen. Whoever wins the round gets a point on the score board. If you and the computer tie, neither gets a point. Whoever wins 2 rounds first wins the game! Click the x to getted started!
